Born February 5, 1985 in Funchal, Madeira — Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro rose from a working-class family on a small Portuguese island to become the most recognizable athlete on the planet. His father worked as a municipal gardener. At 12, he left Madeira to join Sporting CP's youth academy in Lisbon — alone, homesick, determined.

Signed by Manchester United at 18 for £12.24m. Sold to Real Madrid at 24 for a world-record £80m. Today his brand alone is worth hundreds of millions. 5 Ballon d'Or. 5 Champions Leagues. 7 League titles. 130+ international goals. 900+ career goals. No player has dominated across four decades the way he has.

The Beginning

Manchester United
Where Legends Are Born

In 2003, a raw 18-year-old winger from Madeira arrived at Old Trafford and changed football forever. Sir Alex Ferguson saw something extraordinary — pace, skill, and a hunger that nobody could teach. Within five years, Ronaldo had won three Premier League titles, one Champions League, and his first Ballon d'Or.

The 2007/08 season was a masterpiece — 42 goals across all competitions. The Luzhniki header in Moscow sealed the Champions League against Chelsea. The boy from Madeira was 23 and just getting started.

Real Madrid Era

La Liga's Greatest
Scorer of All Time

In 2009, Real Madrid paid a world-record £80 million. They got back 450 goals across nine seasons. He broke Raúl's all-time scoring record. Won La Décima — the long-awaited 10th Champions League. Delivered four UCL titles in five years. The BBC front line (Bale, Benzema, Cristiano) was the most feared attack in European history.

The 2011/12 season produced an unprecedented 50 La Liga goals — a record that still stands. And in 2017, his bicycle kick against Juventus in the UCL earned a standing ovation from the Juventus fans inside their own stadium.

Physical Perfection

Body fat at 7%. Vertical jump of 78cm — exceeding most NBA players. Top speed above 33 km/h. Five 90-minute recovery sleep cycles. Cryotherapy chambers. Personal chef. Zero alcohol. Zero tattoos — so he can donate blood more frequently. Every single detail is engineered for peak performance.

❤️ Their Story in Pictures

Georgina Rodríguez (born January 27, 1994) is an Argentine-Spanish model, entrepreneur, and Netflix star. She met Ronaldo in 2016 at a Gucci store in Madrid where she worked as a sales assistant. A chance encounter between two people with huge dreams — and it changed both their lives forever.

"It is with our deepest sadness we have to announce that our baby boy has passed away. It is the greatest pain that any parents can feel."

— Cristiano & Georgina · April 2022

Together they raise five children — Cristiano Jr., twins Eva & Mateo, Alana Martina, and Bella Esmeralda. On August 11, 2025, Georgina announced their engagement on Instagram with the caption "Yes, I do." Ronaldo revealed it was spontaneous — 1am, daughters asleep, he got down on one knee. Their daughters walked in mid-proposal.

"I always say that good things happen at the right time."

— Cristiano Ronaldo · On the Engagement

The wedding is planned for Summer 2026 at Funchal Cathedral, Madeira — Ronaldo's birthplace — after the FIFA World Cup. Georgina has her own empire: Netflix's I Am Georgina, Vogue/Elle covers, Paris Fashion Week runway, 60M+ Instagram followers, and multiple business ventures.
